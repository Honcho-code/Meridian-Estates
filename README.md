Meridian Estates needed more than a website. They needed a digital salesperson that worked around the clock. The goal was simple: turn curious visitors into paying clients.
The site was built around one priority — removing every barrier between a potential buyer and an enquiry form. A bold hero search bar lets visitors find properties instantly. Listings are filterable, scannable, and each one ends with a direct call to action. The contact form is never more than one click away, no matter where on the page a visitor happens to be.
Every design decision was made with conversion in mind. Trust signals — years in business, properties sold, client satisfaction rates — are placed front and centre before a visitor sees a single listing. The result is a buyer who arrives already confident in the agency before they ever speak to an agent.
On mobile, where the majority of Nigerian property searches happen, the experience is fully optimised. Nothing is hidden. Nothing is broken. Every interaction works cleanly.
The outcome is a website that does not just attract traffic — it captures it. Qualified leads, warm enquiries, and buyers who are already pre-sold by the time the phone rings.
